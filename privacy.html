Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

Note: This is a plain-language template. Please review it (and confirm the operator name and country below) before relying on it.

This policy explains what data My First 100 Foods (myfirst100foods.com), operated by [YOUR FULL NAME OR BUSINESS NAME], collects and how it is used. We keep data collection to a minimum and never sell your personal information.

1. Your tracking data stays on your device

The foods you tick and any custom foods you add are saved only in your browser’s local storage on your own device. This data is not sent to us or stored on any server. Clearing your browser data, or using the “memory code” to move it, is entirely under your control.

2. Analytics & cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand, in aggregate, how the site is used (e.g. visits, which features are used). This is loaded only after you click “Accept” on our cookie banner; if you decline, no analytics cookies are set. IP addresses are anonymized. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing the site’s cookies/storage. See Google’s privacy practices for how they process this data.

3. Messages you send us

If you use the contact form, the email address and message you provide are delivered to our support inbox via the form-processing service FormSubmit so we can reply. We use this only to respond to your enquiry.

4. Purchases

Payments are handled by our Merchant of Record, Lemon Squeezy. When you buy, your payment and billing details are collected and processed by Lemon Squeezy under their own privacy policy — we do not receive or store your card details. The personalized PDF itself is generated in your browser.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold, or to object to processing. Because we hold very little (essentially only support messages you send us), you can exercise these by contacting us. Most of your data lives on your own device and you can delete it yourself at any time.

6. Children

The Service is intended for parents and caregivers, not for use by children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes & contact

We may update this policy and will revise the date above. For any privacy question, use the contact form on the site or the support email listed there. Operator location: [YOUR COUNTRY].
