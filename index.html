Help & answers

Frequently asked questions

When should I start introducing solid foods?
Most pediatric guidelines suggest starting solids around 6 months, once your baby shows signs of readiness: sitting upright with little support, steady head control, curiosity about your food, and no longer pushing food out with their tongue. Every baby is different — always check with your pediatrician before you begin, and when introducing common allergens.
How does this tracker work?
Tap any food to mark it as tried — it turns pastel green and your progress fills the bar at the top and within each category. Use the “+ Add custom” box to add your own foods, or remove ones you don't need. When you're ready, turn your personalized list into a printable tracker with your baby's name.
Where are my ticked foods stored?
Everything is saved privately on your own device, right inside your browser (using local storage). Nothing is uploaded to a server and no account is needed — your progress stays with you.
How do I copy my list to another phone or device?
Open Settings (the gear icon, top right) → “Copy Memory Code”. That copies a short code of your whole list to your clipboard. On the other device, open the site, go to Settings → “Load Saved Code”, and paste it in — your progress and custom foods restore instantly.
In what cases could I lose my data?
Because your list lives in this browser, it can be lost if you clear your browser history / cache or site data, use private / incognito mode, switch to a different browser or device, or uninstall the browser. To be safe, copy your Memory Code (see above) and keep it somewhere safe — that's your backup.
I had a problem with my payment — what should I do?
If your payment didn't go through, or you were charged but didn't receive your tracker, email our support team with the email you used and the approximate time of purchase, and we'll sort it out quickly. Your tracker is also re-generated right after a successful payment, so you can simply try the download again.
